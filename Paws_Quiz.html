Quiz on Making Paws

  • 1. What labels do I use for my pieces?
    • a. A letter, number and arrow
    • b. A letter, number and line marking
    • c. A line marking, number and arrow
    • d. A letter, line marking and arrow

    a. A letter, number and arrow


  • 2. What is seam allowance?
    • a. The part where you add extra room for sewing
    • b. The way that the pattern is made from duct tape
    • c. When you leave the back of the shoe open and close it later
    • d. Covering the shoe in duct tape

    a. The part where you add extra room for sewing


  • 3. The duct tape pieces should be:
    • a. Big
    • b. Small
    • c. Flat
    • d. Bumpy

    c. Flat


  • 4. This project should use approximately:
    • a. 1m of fur
    • b. 0.75m of fur
    • c. 0.5m of fur
    • d. 0.25m of fur

    d. 0.25m of fur


  • 5. Why should you make sure you check where each piece goes?
    • a. Or else the fabric will be mirrored
    • b. Or else you will have to start all over again
    • c. Or else you will have to unpick it
    • d. You don't have to, it doesn't really matter

    c. Or else you will have to unpick it


  • 6. What type of shoe should you use?
    • a. Canvas
    • b. Sneaker
    • c. Boot
    • d. It doesn't matter

    a. Canvas


  • 7. What do you stick the duct tape pieces to?
    • a. The wrong side of the fur
    • b. The right side of the fur
    • c. The newspaper
    • d. The shoe

    d. The shoe


  • 8. Why should you test the foam carving with your fursuit legs before taping?
    • a. To check it's the right shape
    • b. To check it's the same colour
    • c. To check if the duct tape pieces will work
    • d. To label it properly

    a. To check it's the right shape


  • 9. How is the foam attached to the shoe?
    • a. Duct tape
    • b. The fabric holds it in place
    • c. Hot glue
    • d. Shoe glue

    c. Hot glue


  • 10. What colour should the duct tape be?
    • a. Not black
    • b. One colour
    • c. No patterns
    • d. All of the above

    d. All of the above
